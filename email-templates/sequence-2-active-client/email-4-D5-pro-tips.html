3 things our best users do differently

After watching how agencies use DuupFlow, here are the habits that make the biggest difference:

💡 Set up your team before your first big project

Invite your team members in Settings → Team before you start. It's much easier to collaborate from day one than to add people mid-project.

💡 Use one project as your template

Build one "master" project with your standard structure, then duplicate it for every new client. You'll save hours from the second client onward.

💡 Name your projects by client, not by date

Using client names makes it much faster to find things later. Dates change — clients don't.
